## Community Channels

The following links provide information on how to get involved in the community:

- **Slack**: Join the [#kubeflow-trainer](https://app.slack.com/client/T08PSQ7BQ/C0742LDFZ4K) channel on [CNCF Slack](https://slack.cncf.io/).
- **Community Meeting**: Attend the bi-weekly [AutoML and Training Working Group](https://zoom-lfx.platform.linuxfoundation.org/meetings/kubeflow) community meeting.
- **Adopters**: Check out [who is using Kubeflow Trainer](https://github.com/kubeflow/trainer/blob/master/ADOPTERS.md).
